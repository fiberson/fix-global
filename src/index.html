A brief history of cryptocurrency

Ten thousand bitcoin for 2 large pizzas – does it even sound real?

If you think about it now, when crypto is so popular, it sounds crazy. In the years that have passed since then, cryptocurrency has gone a long way.

In February 2011, it hit the $1.06 milestone, rising up to $8.89 in May the same year. The price continued to grow steadily: $434 in January 2016, $998 in January 2017, $20,000 in December 2017.

And it just skyrocketed up to $65,000 in 2021. Crazy if you think it all started with the white paper called “Bitcoin – A Peer To Peer Electronic Cash System” written by the so-called Satoshi Nakamoto.

At the moment, the market is currently expanding, even though the adoption worldwide is only 4.2% (more than 320 million people using cryptocurrency around the world). Alongside the crypto market, the NFTs market evolved as well. It was during the pandemic when NFTs gained a lot of popularity, both for already crypto owners and newcomers to this field.
